Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What happens if I accidentally switch tabs?

A: You'll receive a warning. After 3 warnings, the exam will be terminated.

Q: Can I use a mobile device?

A: No, desktop/laptop with Chrome or Firefox is required.

Q: Is my data secure?

A: Yes, all connections are encrypted and no recordings are stored.

Q: What if I lose internet connection?

A: The system will attempt to reconnect. Prolonged disconnection may end the exam.
